# Phase Model

## Phase 0 — Prelife
Tooling and persistence exist, but no robust fitness view yet.

## Phase 1 — Observability
Differences in behavior and quality become visible.

## Phase 1.5 — Early Proto-Feedback
Strong signals can be generated and prepared for review/adoption.

## Phase 2 — Closed Feedback
Adopted learnings change behavior and their effects are measured later.

## Phase 3 — Controlled Selection
Conservative selection influences defaults or recommendations under audit and guardrails.
